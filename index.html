About Koyuki

Yukio "Koyuki" Tanaka, is a 14-year-old eighth-grade student leading a mundane life, lacking hobbies or a distinct personality. He encounters Ryusuke Minami, an American rock 'n' roll guitarist, leading to significant changes in Koyuki's world.
